Hidden Ability 		Clicking the header row for the Caught column will search for all caught Pokemon. Clicking the header row again will search for uncaught Pokemon. Additionally, you can type in the search bar for caught or uncaught Pokemon by typing "caught" or "!caught" to find caught and uncaught Pokemon respectively. Be sure to include the quotes when searching via text.
And/Or And/Or logic can be used when searching. To search with and logic (for instance any Pokemon that are both Fire and Water type) you must use the & syntax. Thus to search for any Pokemon that both Fire and Water you would type Fire & Water into the search bar.

To search with and logic (for instance any Pokemon that are both Fire and Water type) you must use the & syntax. Thus to search for any Pokemon that both Fire and Water you would type Fire & Water into the search bar.
